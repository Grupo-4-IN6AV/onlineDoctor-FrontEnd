Our History

We welcome you to Online Doctor, your trusted Web Hospital, our main objective is to ensure your health and well-being. We are delighted to serve you.

We are an organization founded in 2022 by developers and specialized doctors in Guatemala, interested in forming a society dedicated to professional and specialized medical practice. As an organization we have set ourselves the goal of creating a Virtual Hospital that will be dedicated to treating all patients registered in our application quickly and efficiently.
